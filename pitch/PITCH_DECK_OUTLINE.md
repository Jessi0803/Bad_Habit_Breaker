## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: Purple (#667eea)
- Secondary: Pink/Magenta (#764ba2)
- Accent: Gold (#FFD700) for highlights
- Text: White on dark backgrounds
- Background: Dark gradients

### Fonts
- Headlines: Bold, modern sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Roboto, Open Sans)
- Code: Monospace (Fira Code, Consolas)

### Visual Style
- Modern, playful but professional
- Generous white space
- High contrast for readability
- Consistent icon style
- Partner logos prominent but tasteful

## Presentation Tips

### Timing
- Slide 1: 5 seconds (hook)
- Slide 2: 15 seconds (problem)
- Slide 3: 10 seconds (solution intro)
- Slide 4-Demo: 60 seconds (LIVE DEMO)
- Slide 5: 10 seconds (how it works)
- Slide 6: 15 seconds (features)
- Slide 7: 10 seconds (tech/partners)
- Slide 8: 10 seconds (differentiation)
- Slide 9: 15 seconds (market/impact)
- Slide 10: 10 seconds (CTA)

**Total: ~2 minutes** (leave time for Q&A)

### Delivery
- **Don't read slides** - slides are visual aids
- **Tell a story** - problem â†’ solution â†’ impact
- **Be enthusiastic** - if you're not excited, why should judges be?
- **Make eye contact** with each judge
- **Pause** after key points
- **Show, don't tell** - hence the demo!
