## Q&A Preparation

### Expected Questions & Answers

**Q: "How do you detect 'bad' behavior?"**
> A: "Currently rule-based - time thresholds per domain. But we're building an LLM-powered version that detects patterns like rapid scrolling, excessive clicking, etc."

**Q: "Can I customize which sites to monitor?"**
> A: "Absolutely. You can add any domain with custom thresholds. Want to limit YouTube to 10 minutes? Done."

**Q: "Privacy concerns - are you tracking my data?"**
> A: "All monitoring happens locally in your browser. Nothing sent to our servers unless you opt-in for cloud sync."

**Q: "How do you use ElevenLabs?"**
> A: "Voice generation for interventions. You can clone your mom's voice, your favorite celebrity, or use our pre-made personalities."

**Q: "What makes this different from screen time apps?"**
> A: "Traditional blockers are binary - allow or block. We're conversational. The AI *talks* to you, makes it personal. Way harder to ignore your mom's voice than a popup."

**Q: "How do you use Clerk/Bolt/other partners?"**
> A: "Clerk handles authentication for cross-device sync. Bolt deploys our backend instantly. All integrations make this production-ready, not just a prototype."

**Q: "Can I really clone my mom's voice?"**
> A: "Yes! ElevenLabs requires just 30 seconds of audio. Imagine the guilt trip potential!" *[Laugh]*

**Q: "Will this work on other browsers?"**
> A: "Currently Chrome only, but Firefox and Edge use similar APIs. We can port it quickly."

**Q: "How do you monetize?"**
> A: "Freemium model. Basic version free, premium features like custom voices, advanced analytics, team accounts for companies."

## Presentation Tips

### Do's âœ…
- **Speak clearly and slowly** (you'll want to rush)
- **Make eye contact** with judges
- **Smile** - show enthusiasm!
- **Point at screen** when showing features
- **Pause** after the voice plays (let it sink in)
- **Tell a story** (personal anecdote)
- **Show personality** (have fun!)

### Don'ts âŒ
- Don't apologize for bugs (unless critical)
- Don't read from slides
- Don't go over time
- Don't use jargon without explaining
- Don't forget to mention partners
- Don't panic if something breaks
